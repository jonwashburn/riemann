5.2. Phase–velocity identity and positivity
You want, per Whitney interval I:
\text{windowed_phase}(I) = \pi \, \text{poisson_balayage}(I) + \pi \, \text{critical_atoms}(I),
with critical_atoms(I) ≥ 0.

Conceptually:
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * Consider the argument of J along a rectangular contour hugging the boundary segment over I plus some height.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * Use Green’s theorem to relate the boundary integral of arg J to a bulk integral of Δ(arg J), which is concentrated at zeros and poles.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * For an analytic function without poles in Ω, zeros contribute positive multiples of π to the phase increment (argument principle). That’s the atomic part.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     * The harmonic measure of I w.r.t. the box is your Poisson balayage term.
