-------------------------

   - Defines trace-class operators and Fredholm determinants
   - Product formula for diagonal operators

2. Diagonal Operator Analysis  
   - Evolution operator A(s) = diag(p^{-s})
   - Eigenvalue analysis and summability conditions

3. Euler Product Connection
   - Links det₂(I - A(s)) to 1/ζ(s)
   - Analytic continuation from Re(s) > 1 to critical strip

4. Birman-Schwinger Principle
   - ζ(s) = 0 ⟺ det₂(I - A(s)) = 0 ⟺ 1 ∈ spectrum(A(s))
   - Eigenvalue 1 means some p^{-s} = 1

5. Spectral Stability
   - Key insight: p^{-s} = 1 ⟹ |p^{-s}| = p^{-Re(s)} = 1
   - Since p ≥ 2, this forces Re(s) = 0
   - Contradiction in critical strip where 1/2 < Re(s) < 1

6. Main Theorem
   - Combines all pieces to prove RH
   - Handles trivial zeros separately
   - Uses functional equation for Re(s) < 1/2
